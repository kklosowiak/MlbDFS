## What Was Tested

For each game in the 2026 MLB season, the backtest simulated a
'physics-based stack call' using DQI gates:

1. **Team xwOBA >= floor** (offensive quality gate)
2. **Opposing SP physics <= cap** (vulnerable pitcher gate)

A call fires when BOTH gates pass simultaneously (AND logic).
Success = team scores 4+ or 5+ actual runs in the game.
